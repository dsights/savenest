Current State of SaveNest.au
What They Do Well:
Multi-utility platform - Covers electricity, gas, internet, mobile, health insurance, car insurance, and finance
Clean, modern design - Professional branding with good visual hierarchy
Trust indicators - Shows major brand logos (AGL, Westpac, CommBank, ANZ, etc.), verified customer testimonials with 5-star ratings
Value proposition - Clear messaging: "100% Secure", "Top Rated Providers", "Saves you hours", "100% Free"
Referral program - Earn $50 for every friend referred
Content marketing - Active blog with energy-saving tips and industry news
State-specific guides - NSW, VIC, QLD, SA Energy Guides
Current Limitations:
Flutter/Web rendering - The site uses Flutter for web, which makes it less accessible for screen readers and harder to interact with programmatically
Limited visible interactivity - The homepage calculator widget shows static $0 savings estimates
Generic comparison results - Insurance page shows deals but limited customization visible
No visible advanced filtering - Basic search and tags (cheap, best, new, vic, qld)
RECOMMENDATIONS: Making SaveNest the BEST Interactive Utility Comparison Site
1. ENHANCED COMPARISON EXPERIENCE
Interactive Calculator Improvements:

Smart Defaults - Pre-populate based on user location (IP-based state detection)
Visual Sliders - Interactive sliders for usage amounts (kWh for electricity, GB for internet)
Real-time Estimates - Show live savings calculations as users adjust inputs
Comparison Scenarios - "Light User", "Average Family", "Heavy User" presets
Multi-utility Bundling - Show combined savings when switching multiple services
Advanced Filtering & Sorting:

Dynamic filters: Green energy %, contract length, exit fees, discounts, payment methods
Smart sorting: Best for seniors, families, renters, homeowners
Deal badges: "New customer bonus", "No lock-in", "Price guarantee", "Best for solar"
