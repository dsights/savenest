# SaveNest Solar: Paid Ad Strategy (Day 6 Strike)

## 1. Facebook & Instagram Ads (Visual & Emotional)
**Goal:** Disrupt the scroll of homeowners complaining about electricity bills.

### Campaign 1: The "Lazy Tax" Angle
*   **Creative:** A split-screen image. Left side: A dusty, old electricity bill with "OVERDUE" or high amount. Right side: A clean, /usr/bin/bash solar bill.
*   **Headline:** Stop paying the Australian "Lazy Tax" on electricity. 🛑
*   **Primary Text:** Most [State] homeowners are paying 25% more than they should just because they haven't checked their solar eligibility in 2026. The new government rebates cover up to [Amount] of the cost. 
*   **Call to Action:** Learn More (Link to SaveNest Solar Calculator).

### Campaign 2: The "Rebate Alert" (Highest Conversion)
*   **Creative:** A selfie video or a map of Australia with a green checkmark over it.
*   **Headline:** Is your address eligible for the 2026 Solar Rebate? 📍
*   **Primary Text:** Government solar incentives have changed. See if your roof qualifies for a /usr/bin/bash out-of-pocket installation. It takes 30 seconds to check.
*   **Call to Action:** Check Eligibility.

## 2. Google Search Ads (High Intent)
**Goal:** Capture people actively searching for solutions.

### Ad Group: Solar Quotes
*   **Keywords:** "best solar panels sydney", "solar quotes melbourne", "solar rebate calculator", "cheap solar installer [suburb]".
*   **Headline 1:** SaveNest™ | Official 2026 Solar Check
*   **Headline 2:** /usr/bin/bash Upfront Solar for Homeowners
*   **Description:** Stop overpaying. We compare the top CEC-accredited installers in [City]. Check your state rebate eligibility in 30 seconds.

## 3. Retargeting (The Closer)
**Goal:** Bring back people who visited the site but didn't submit a lead.
