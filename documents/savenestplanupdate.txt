1. The "Incompleteness" Audit: What’s Likely MissingMost Australian comparison sites feel incomplete because they require too much manual entry. To move beyond this, your UX needs to shift from Input-Heavy to Zero-Touch.The "Bill Upload" Friction: If I have to type in my NMI (National Meter Identifier) or my current cents/kWh, I’ll drop off.The "Switching" Cliff: Most sites redirect you to the provider’s website, where you have to start the application from scratch. This is where you lose the "multi-million dollar" value.The "Post-Switch" Ghosting: A true "Nest" should monitor the market after the switch and alert the user when their "honeymoon rate" expires.2. The Tech Stack for "Autonomous Switching"To enable the functionality of an "Active Agent," you should leverage the Consumer Data Right (CDR) and Agentic AI.A. The Data Plumbing (Consumer Data Right)Don't build a scraper. Use the official Australian government framework.Energy & Banking APIs: Register as an Accredited Data Recipient (ADR) or use a "CDR Representative" model (easier/cheaper) via providers like Adatree or Basiq.The Benefit: Instead of asking for bill details, the user clicks "Connect my Energy Provider." You instantly receive their exact usage profile, current plan, and solar export data. This allows for 100% accurate price comparisons.B. The Intelligence Layer (Agentic AI)Orchestration: Use LangChain or AutoGPT frameworks with Gemini 1.5 Pro or GPT-4o.The Task: The AI doesn't just rank plans; it reads the "Fine Print" (PDFs) of Energy Fact Sheets to find hidden fees or "late payment" penalties that a database might miss.Logic Separation: Keep your "Switching Logic" deterministic. Use the AI to recommend, but use a hard-coded rules engine to validate the math.C. The "Ghost" Action LayerBrowser Automation (Playwright/Puppeteer): Until providers offer "Switching APIs," you can use "headless browsers" to pre-fill the application forms for the user.Human-in-the-loop: For the first 1,000 users, have a concierge verify the AI’s work before the "Switch" button is finalized.3.
